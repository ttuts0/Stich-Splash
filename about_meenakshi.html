My Story

I was taught needle point and other fine arts by my mother very early in life. I love doing things with my hand and it gives me great delight to see the end results, but the real joy is in the process. content_cut

Today, I embrace a philosophy of "happy accidents." In my studio, there are no mistakes, only unexpected design opportunities. Whether we're exploring acrylics or sewing a new pair of pants, the goal is always joy and authentic expression.
